Welcome to StoreSecured.com
On Your Statement

Charges for your easy website builder, hosting and added value services will show on your credit card statement as "StoreSecured.com".  You may see a fee for custom work such as quick starts, merchant account setups, or template design and then a low monthly fee in the following months.

If you do not recognize your charge, please call, email or chat with us and we will be happy to help out.

Receipts

You can access all receipts for your website directly from your website builder.  To find the receipts, log into your website, and click on "My Account" from your toolbar and then click on the "Prior Invoices" link.

Canceling Your Store

We want you to be happy while you're with us and if you don't need your store anymore we want to make it easy for you to leave.  To cancel your site, simply log into your store and under the "My Account" area select the Cancel Store link.  Here you will see an area for you to cancel your site. Once you click the cancel link, you will not receive another charge for your store and your store will be taken offline and completely removed.

Changing Billing Information

Changing your billing information is easy and can be done online.  Simply log into your store and click on "My Account" and then click on the "Update Payment" link.

Billing Support

If you have a question regarding any charges you are receiving from StoreSecured.com on your credit card statement, please contact us via email, phone or chat.
